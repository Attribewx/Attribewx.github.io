What I Do

Game Developer

I've been making games ever since I learned how for a Local Video Game Competition called Skoolcade in 2018. At the time I didn't know anything about C# but I learned a lot in two weeks because thats when the deadline was and I think I made a pretty decent game in that time. The only problem with it was that it was too hard and I learned that I should tone back the difficulty even if that means its easy for me. However I didn't let that failure be the end of my dream, I kept working at my new found craft and made it my goal to win next years competition with flying colors and I did just that.

Artist

Ever since I was a little kid I loved making things, I wasn't very good at it and I still don't think I'm that good but I think with enough practice you can do anything. I believe in my ability to learn new things and improve on what I have learned. I mean I made this website and I definitely didn't know much about website design before this. I like to think that I'm a newbie at everything I do, so that I can always look for something to learn and keep growing in whatever I'm doing.

Video Editor

I love editing videos, I only really do it for fun but thats honestly why I do anything! I usually just make youtube videos of me and my friends playing video games or just a montage of some cool or stupid plays. I also made a trailer for my game Wave Jumper and it came out alright I think for making it in less than an hour.

Who I am

This is me (On the left)

I love creating things! It doesn't matter what it is if I'm making it I'll enjoy my time making it. Its so much fun learning things on the way to your finished product cause you can look back and just see your growth from the beginning and see where you can improve. If I'm not making something I'm probably learning how to make something or just learning something.

I have been very interested in video games ever since I could play them, which was when I turned three. I think they've really shaped who I am today. They sent me down a path that I got very interested in which was just computer science in general. I've learned so many things that seem difficult but once you dip your feet in, you find out its pretty simple.

Some of my favorite games include: Metroid, Darks Souls, Ratchet and Clank, Hollow Knight, Overwatch, Apex Legends, Rocket League, The Legend of Zelda, Minecraft, Little Big Planet, and too many more.

If there was one thing I liked more than games it would definitely be Magic. Not of the gathering, or card tricks type though. But just like Avatar The Last Airbender kinda magic, or something like an RPG's Magic. Just something that looks pretty and does something really cool. I like magic because its not something of this world but with some art and engineering you can get something close to it and I think thats just absolutely incredible.

About Me

I love a good challenge. If something is difficult then I will definitely enjoy it because it puts your brain to a test. That test is usually to change your way of thinking of the problem, because no one ever got anywhere doing the same exact thing everytime. Whenever I am challenged by a difficult problem I always try my hardest to match its difficulty with my skill.

I am probably way too curious for my own good. If I don't know how something works or what something is I will take the time to learn about it if it seems interesting or that it could be useful to know. This is definitely why I am interested in art because I love observing how things work and why they work which helps a lot with drawing weird or just normal things like a simple run animation for a rabbit.
